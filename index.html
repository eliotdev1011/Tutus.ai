Why TutusAI ?

Privacy By Design

TutusAI uses a Privacy-by-design methodology in its core and ensures minimal data retention and strict access controls as well as an ability to manage and delete data.

Fully Homomorphic Encryption

TutusAI utilizes Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE) elements, enabling an unmatched level of data security. AI model training while maintaining user privacy, unlike competitors.

Platform like no other

Confidential Machine Learning (ConfML) and Tendermint-based consensus is employed in PGPT to achieve a decentralized, privacy-preserving AI platform by ensuring secure data handling and consensus among nodes.
